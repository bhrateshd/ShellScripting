What is Shell Scripting?


Shell Scripting is a powerful way to automate and streamline tasks within a Linux envirnoment by writting executing commands.
Its invaluable for system administration, task automation, and enhancing productivity.

Types of Shells: Some popular shells include:

	bash (Bourne Again Shell) - most widely used
	sh(Bourne Shell)
	ksh(KornShell)
	zsh(Z Shell)
	
Check Your Shell Type: Run the below command to identify your current shell:

	echo $0
